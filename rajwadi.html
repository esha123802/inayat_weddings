ROYAL RAJWADI

You’ve been waiting forever for that grand wedding. Get married in classy way and feel like a royal couple on your glorious wedding. For a royal wedding, nothing less than a classic palace would do, as your wedding venue. Find a heritage property, a hotel resort, an outdoor location or even a popular destination wedding location. Make bridal or groom’s entries with traditional grand announcements with those shahnai and band wallas. You can go for live Nagada players instead of a DJ to fit in the theme. Make sure you add a touch of grandiose in menu too. Talk to your wedding caterer while deciding on the wedding food and include food and drinks apt for a royal themed wedding.
